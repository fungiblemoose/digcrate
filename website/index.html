Features

Your library, analyzed and arranged.

Scanning, analysis, and previews are free out of the box. AI planning and the tools that turn a tracklist into a finished set are a one-time DigCrate Pro purchase — no subscription.

Free, out of the box

Library analysis

Point DigCrate at a folder and it reads BPM, musical key in 8A-style harmonic notation, and energy for every track — all on your Mac.

Track previews

Audition any track from a smart cue point that drops you near the part that matters, instead of the silence at the top.

Review queue

Tracks with shaky analysis get flagged. Fix a BPM or key by hand, confirm the rest, and keep your library clean.

Search & stats

Filter by BPM, key, or energy, run a text search, and see your whole library at a glance — counts, ranges, and most-common keys.

DigCrate Pro

Pro one-time purchase

AI set planning

Describe the set in plain English — “60-minute liquid roller, mellow start, peak around minute 40” — and the planner picks and orders tracks for flow.

Transition scoring

Every handoff is scored on the harmonic wheel, with a plain-language note on why it works — or why it clashes.

Gap analysis

Weak transitions get flagged with the BPM, key, and energy of the bridge track that would smooth them over.

Bridge-track discovery

One click opens Beatport, Spotify, or SoundCloud searches built from the gap’s profile, so you know exactly what to hunt for.

Rekordbox & M3U export

Export a finished set as Rekordbox XML or an M3U playlist with metadata intact, ready to load into the booth.

How it works

From a folder to a finished set.

DigCrate moves through the same steps you would, just faster — and keeps you in control at every one.

1

Scan

Point at a folder. DigCrate analyzes each track for BPM, key, and energy on your Mac.

2

Plan

Describe the set you want. The planner drafts a tracklist ordered for harmonic flow.

3

Review

Preview tracks, read the transition scores, reorder by hand, and lock the ones you’re keeping.

4

Fill gaps

Weak transitions surface with a suggested BPM and key for the bridge track that fixes them.

5

Discover

Jump to store searches built from each gap to track down the records you’re missing.

6

Export

Send the finished set to Rekordbox XML or M3U and load it straight into your decks.
