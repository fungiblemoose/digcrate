// Features

Everything stays on your machine.

No uploads, no API keys required. DeepCrate runs entirely on your Mac with local models for AI planning.

♫

Library Analysis

Scans your folders and detects BPM, musical key (Camelot), and energy level for every track. Fix metadata inline.

⚙

AI Set Planning

Describe the vibe in plain English. DeepCrate picks tracks, orders them for flow, and scores every transition.

🎧

Harmonic Mixing

Transitions scored against the Camelot wheel. Green means clean, red means clash. No guessing.

🔍

Gap Discovery

Flags weak transitions and suggests the BPM, key, and energy of a bridge track that would fix it.

🎵

Spotify Search

One-click search for tracks matching gap criteria. Build a shortlist of fills without leaving the app.

📦

Rekordbox Export

Export sets as Rekordbox XML or M3U playlists. Metadata preserved. Ready for the booth.

// Workflow

Six tabs. One session.

From raw folders to an exportable set in minutes. Each step builds on the last.

1

Scan

Point at a folder. DeepCrate analyzes every track for BPM, key, and energy.

2

Plan

Describe your set in plain English. The AI picks tracks, orders them, and scores transitions.

3

Review

Preview your set. See transition scores. Drag to reorder. Lock tracks you like.

4

Fill Gaps

Weak transitions are flagged with suggested BPM/key. Search Spotify for fills.

5

Discover

Browse candidates, save a shortlist, chase down the tracks that complete your set.

6

Export

Rekordbox XML or M3U. Metadata intact. Load it up and play.
